WHAT IS FRIDAY FIVE O'CLOCK?

It all began as a hobby, with a passion for crafting cocktails from my own kitchen, and now, every Friday, I bring you a brand-new, beautiful, and oh-so-tasty cocktail recipe. The mission is simple: to inspire you to become your own mixologist. You don't need a fancy bar or a long list of ingredients to create something magical. Just everyday items and a dash of creativity!

Join the community for a flavor-packed journey as I show you that the art of cocktails is for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned cocktail enthusiast or a complete novice, there's always something delightful to sip on at Friday Five o'Clock. It's all about celebrating the joy of home mixology, one cocktail at a time. Cheers to making Fridays even more enjoyable every week!

TOP THREE EASY-TO-MAKE COCKTAILS

NEGRONI

    Ingredients:

  • 30 ml gin
  • 30 ml sweet vermouth
  • 30 ml Campari

Method:

Mix all the ingredients and stir with a lot of ice. Add an orange peel for garnish.
